A small camp with room for a big experience.

Camp Lawton combines six daily merit badge blocks with outdoor skills, campwide challenges, evening programs, and the close-knit character of a century-old mountain camp.

Read before arrival

Four details that change how you pack and plan

  • No aquatics: Camp Lawton has no pool or waterfront.
  • Carry-in gear: Vehicles remain in the designated parking area.
  • Fire restrictions: Conditions can change without warning; always plan a no-flame alternative.
  • Medications: Prescription medications go to the Camp Health Officer at check-in.
